About us

Welcome to Kimchi Nachos, where we blend the vibrant flavors of Korean cuisine with the beloved tradition of tacos! Based in Dublin, Ireland, our mission is to deliver high-quality, delicious meals that cater to all tastes and preferences while maintaining a commitment to sustainability and affordability.

At Kimchi Tacos, we prioritize quality above all else. We source our ingredients from local suppliers who share our commitment to sustainability. Our fresh produce and meats are carefully selected to ensure that every bite is packed with flavor and nutrition. We are proud to use traditional Korean ingredients like gochujang (Korean chili paste) and homemade kimchi, alongside locally sourced vegetables and proteins. This dedication to quality means you can enjoy our dishes knowing they are made with love and care.

Our Story

Founded by a passionate team of food enthusiasts, Kimchi Tacos was born out of a love for culinary fusion. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a unique dining experience that marries the bold, spicy flavors of Korea with the comforting familiarity of tacos. We believe that food is not just about nourishment; it’s about bringing people together and creating memorable moments. With this vision in mind, we set out to craft a menu that reflects our diverse backgrounds and culinary passions.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to offer authentic Korean dishes with a twist,all in the form of convenient tacos.
