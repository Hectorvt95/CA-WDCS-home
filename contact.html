Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are Kimchi Nachos?

Kimchi Nachos are a fusion dish combining the bold, tangy flavors of Korean kimchi with the classic cheesy, crunchy elements of traditional nachos. Our version includes crispy tortilla chips, melted cheese, spicy kimchi, and a variety of toppings like Korean BBQ, green onions, and sesame seeds.

2. Can I customize my Kimchi Nachos?

Yes! You can choose from our selection of proteins (like chicken, beef, pork, or tofu) and add extra toppings such as jalapeños, avocado, or extra cheese for an additional charge.

3. Are the Kimchi Nachos spicy?

Our Kimchi Nachos have a mild-to-medium spice level. If you prefer less heat, we can tone down the spice by reducing the kimchi or spicy sauce. Let us know your preference when placing your order.

4. How do I place an order?

You can order online through our website, on food delivery apps like Uber Eats and DoorDash, or by calling us directly.
